Chapati

Chapati

Chapati Recipe

  • 2 cups whole wheat flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup water (approx.)
  • 2 tablespoons oil

Instructions:

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine the whole wheat flour and salt.
  2. Add water gradually and knead the dough until it becomes soft and pliable. Add more water if needed.
  3. Divide the dough into small, equal-sized balls.
  4. Take a dough ball and dust it with flour. Roll it out into a thin, round circle.
  5. Heat a tawa or flat pan on medium heat.
  6. Place the rolled chapati on the hot pan and cook for about 30 seconds or until bubbles start to appear.
  7. Flip the chapati and cook the other side for another 30 seconds.
  8. Apply a little oil on both sides of the chapati and cook for a few more seconds until it turns golden brown.
  9. Remove the chapati from the pan and repeat the process with the remaining dough balls.
  10. Serve the chapatis hot with your favorite curry or dish.

Cheese cake

Cheese cake

Cheesecake Recipe

  • 2 cups graham cracker crumbs
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 3 (8 oz) packages cream cheese, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 325°F (163°C).
  2. In a bowl, mix the graham cracker crumbs and melted butter until well combined.
  3. Press the crumb mixture into the bottom of a 9-inch springform pan to form the crust.
  4. In a large mixing bowl, beat the cream cheese, sugar, and vanilla extract until smooth and creamy.
  5. Add the eggs, one at a time, beating well after each addition.
  6. Stir in the sour cream and flour until just combined.
  7. Pour the cream cheese mixture over the crust in the springform pan.
  8. Smooth the top with a spatula.
  9. Bake in the preheated oven for about 60-70 minutes or until the center is set and the top is lightly golden.
  10. Remove the cheesecake from the oven and let it cool in the pan for about 10 minutes.
  11. Run a knife around the edges to loosen the cheesecake, then remove the sides of the springform pan.
  12. Let the cheesecake cool completely, then refrigerate for at least 4 hours or overnight before serving.
  13. Optional: Serve topped with your favorite fruit topping or whipped cream.

Sandwich

Sandwich

Sandwich Recipe

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 4-6 slices of your favorite deli meat (e.g., turkey, ham, chicken)
  • 2 slices of cheese (e.g., cheddar, Swiss, provolone)
  • Lettuce leaves
  • Sliced tomatoes
  • Mayonnaise or mustard (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Take two slices of bread and lay them flat on a clean surface.
  2. Spread mayonnaise or mustard on one or both slices of bread, if desired.
  3. Layer the deli meat on one slice of bread, followed by the cheese slices.
  4. Add lettuce leaves and sliced tomatoes on top of the cheese.
  5. Sprinkle with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Place the second slice of bread on top to form a sandwich.
  7. Press the sandwich gently to ensure the ingredients stick together.
  8. Cut the sandwich in half diagonally or into quarters, if desired.
  9. Serve the sandwich immediately or wrap it for later enjoyment.
